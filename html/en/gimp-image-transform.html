6.14. Transform

Figure 16.70.  The “Transform” submenu of the “Image” menu


The items on the Transform submenu transform the image by flipping it, rotating it or cropping it.

6.14.1. Activating the Submenu

  • You can access this submenu from the image menubar through Image → Transform.

6.14.2. The Contents of the “Transform” Submenu

The Transform submenu has the following commands:

  • Flip Horizontally; Flip Vertically

  • Rotate 90° clockwise / counter-clockwise; Rotate 180°

  • Slice Using Guides
