4.5. Noise Reduction

4.5.1. Overview

A simple GEGL filter to reduce noise.

4.5.2. Activating the filter

You can find this filter through Filters → Enhance → Noise Reduction….

4.5.3. Options

Figure 17.32. Noise Reduction filter options


Presets, Preview, Split view

These options are common to GEGL-based filters. Please refer to Section 2, “Common Features”.

Strength

Increasing Strength lessens noise, and increases blur: find compromise using the on-canvas preview.
